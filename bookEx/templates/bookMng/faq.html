Welcome to the FAQ Page!



Add a book by going to Post Book. Be sure you have a website, price, and photo of the books' front cover. Once you have these added to the form, click on the 'Post' button.

Click on 'Search Books' in the navigation bar at the top. This tool can be used to find any book in our database and is very powerful. You can try to search for your book by full name, or by typing in parts of the title you know.
For example if you are looking for 'Harry Potter', you can type that as a whole, or type in 'potter' and the search page will find it for you, as well as any other book with 'potter' in the title.

The "About Us" page is currently used for giving credit to all the developers of this website project and allows you, the user, to see who took part in this. As well as promoting the GitHub of each creator.

The 'My Books' page is there to see what books you have posted. If you have not posted a book then you will not see any there. Books you have bought or have on your wishlist will also not show up there.
